What is n8n and Why Use It?

n8n (pronounced n-eight-n) is a fair-code licensed workflow automation tool that helps you connect any app with an API with any other, and manipulate its data with little or no code. Our instance focuses specifically on Google services integration, providing a secure, self-hosted environment where you can:

  • Create automated workflows between your favorite Google applications
  • Process emails, documents, and spreadsheets automatically
  • Save time by automating repetitive business processes
  • Connect different services together in a visual, easy-to-understand interface
  • Keep complete control over your data with our self-hosted solution
  • Build multi-step AI agents and integrate over 500 applications

Whether you're looking to automate your email responses, synchronize data between applications, or process information from multiple sources, n8n provides the tools you need in a secure, privacy-focused environment.

The fast way to actually get AI working in your business

With n8n, you can build multi-step agents calling custom tools and integrate any LLM into your workflows as fast as you can drag-n-drop. Self-host everything—including AI models—to protect your data by deploying on-premises.

Code When You Need It, UI When You Don't

Unlike other tools that limit you to either a visual building experience or code, n8n gives you the best of both worlds:

  • Write JavaScript or Python when needed
  • Add libraries from npm or Python
  • Paste cURL requests into your workflow
  • Merge workflow branches, don't just split them

Run. Tweak. Repeat.
